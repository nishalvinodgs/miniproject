Our Story

Founded with a vision to democratize luxury, thriftIN emerged as India's premier destination for authenticated pre-owned designer goods and premium electronics.

We recognized that luxury shouldn't be disposable. Every Gucci bag, every PlayStation 5, every pair of premium sneakers has stories to tell and years of life ahead.

Today, we're proud to be the trusted platform where luxury meets sustainability, connecting discerning customers with authenticated designer pieces and cutting-edge electronics.

Our Philosophy

At thriftIN, we believe luxury and sustainability can coexist beautifully. Every item undergoes rigorous authentication by our certified experts.

We're not just a marketplace – we're curators of exceptional pre-owned luxury, making premium accessible while reducing environmental impact.
